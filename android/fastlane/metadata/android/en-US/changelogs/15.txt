Initial release of Tibane.

- Non-custodial Solana wallet, with optional Mobile Wallet Adapter for Seeker and Saga.
- Browse and join ChiefStaker staking pools.
- Incinerator: burn empty token accounts, junk NFTs, and unused .sol domains to reclaim SOL.
- Token info: supply, market cap, burned amount, top holders.
- Built-in Solana dApp browser.

Tibane gives Solana power users a single, fast app for everything that matters: a non-custodial wallet, a staking explorer with live pool stats, an account incinerator that reclaims SOL from dust accounts, and a token info tool with on-chain holders, market cap, and supply.

WALLET
- Non-custodial. Your keys live on your device.
- Built first for Solana Mobile (Seeker, Saga) with Mobile Wallet Adapter.
- Also runs as a self-contained in-app wallet — no external app required.
- Live SOL and SPL token balances, with portfolio totals and recent transfers.

STAKING POOLS
- Browse every ChiefStaker pool — total staked, rewards, member count.
- Dive into a pool's full stats: pool weight, decay (tau), milestones.
- Track your own stake, weight progression, and pending rewards.

INCINERATOR
- Find empty token accounts, junk NFTs, and unused .sol domains.
- Burn them to reclaim the SOL rent (about 0.002 SOL per account).
- Sponsored mode: a relay pays the network fee, you keep most of the rent.
- Adjustable tip — share rent with the relay or keep nearly all of it.

TOKEN INFO
- Look up any SPL token: supply, market cap, burned amount, decimals.
- Top holders with on-chain percentages.
- Mint address with one-tap copy.

DAPP BROWSER
- Launch any Solana dApp from the in-app browser.
- Sign with the wallet you already have set up.

Open source. Built by Tibane Labs — a Solana research team named after the cat that started it all.
